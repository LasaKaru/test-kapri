Built for the Last Stand

🌲

Living, Huntable Wild

A procedural forest of wind-swayed trees, flowers, ferns & mushrooms — roamed by deer, boar, roosters and wolves you can hunt for meat & loot to eat, sell or craft.

🎯

Pure Gunplay

Tight raycast shooting, recoil, reloads and a satisfying KILL punch on every confirmed takedown — across an arsenal of nine weapons.

🌊

Endless Waves

Each wave grows hungrier and faster. Push your score, your accuracy and your nerve as far as they'll go.

👥

Multiplayer Co-op

Quick Match or host/join by code, then fight one shared wave together — shared score, spreading aggro, and a unique callsign floating above every squadmate.

🚀

Vehicles & Secrets

Type secret codes to deploy a rideable tank, bike or jet — then fly out and bomb the fortified enemy base.

🗺

Missions & Checkpoints

Pick your battlefield and difficulty, advance a 24-mission campaign, and Continue from your furthest wave instead of starting over.

🎬

Cinematic Presentation

A looping camera flythrough behind the menu, dynamic day/night & weather, bloom and a warm filmic grade you can tune with the Realism slider.

Take the Field

Know your Threats

Crimson Grunt

Slow, relentless and everywhere. The backbone of every wave. Two clean shots and it's done.

Scorched Runner

Fast and fragile. It will close the gap before you can blink. Lead your shots.

Ember Brute

A wall of muscle that soaks up rounds. Appears in later waves to break your rhythm.

The Arsenal

🔫

KR-15 Rifle

The reliable all-rounder. Fully automatic, balanced damage and a steady 30-round magazine.

💨

V-9 SMG

Blistering fire rate and a deep mag. Spray down runners up close — accuracy drops at range.

💥

BR-2 Breacher

A nine-pellet shotgun that deletes anything in your face. Reward for letting them get close.

🎯

LR-7 Marksman

Scoped, one-shot authority. Aim down sights to zoom and drop brutes before they reach you.

Nine weapons in all — also a SD-9 Sidearm, a belt-fed HG-50 Gatling, an MK-8 DMR, a full-auto AA-12 and the ARC-9 Railgun (plus a secret RPG-7 for those who know the code). Swap freely with 1–9 or the scroll wheel; each keeps its own ammo and levels up as you rack up kills. Land headshots for bonus damage, melee with V, and learn each gun's recoil pattern. Lob frag grenades with G, find medkits, armor and ammo dropped by the fallen, and shoot the red explosive barrels to clear a crowd. Between waves, spend credits at the shop on resupply and permanent perks — Vitality, Fast Hands, Lifesteal and more.
