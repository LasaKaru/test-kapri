# Third-party assets

VERDANT uses the following third-party assets. Thank you to the creators.

## 3D models

- **Medieval Fantasy Book** — used as an in-world landmark (`assets/models/medieval.glb`).
  - Author: **Pixel** (https://sketchfab.com/stefan.lengyel1)
  - Source: https://sketchfab.com/3d-models/medieval-fantasy-book-06d5a80a04fc4c5ab552759e9a97d91a
  - License: **CC-BY-4.0** (http://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/)
  - Changes: re-scaled, re-oriented and placed as a static landmark; no mesh changes.

All other models, textures and audio in VERDANT are generated procedurally in code.
